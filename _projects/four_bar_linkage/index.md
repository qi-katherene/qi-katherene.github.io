The goal of this project was to design a potential mini golf obstacle using a 4 bar mechanism.
The prototype is to be made of acrylic. <br><br>

There were 9 awards out of 40+ groups in the course. This design was voted as "Most Mini Golf Award." 
Below is a schematic to illustrate the idea of the obstacle design. The moving bridge is the 4 bar mechanism that will act as a moving path for golfers to decide if they want to use it to reach the target hole. <br><br>
